🥳🥳 Happy Bhai Dooj! 🥳🥳

Celebrate the special bond between brothers and sisters.

🎉🎉 Bhai Dooj is a festival celebrated by siblings to exchange gifts and shower each other with love. The word "Bhai" means brother and "Dooj" means the second day after the new moon. 🎉🎉

Thank you for being my pillars of strength and my constant. I pray to God that you reach the pinnacle of success and the zenith of glory. May the love and affection between us be everlasting. On this special day, I want to express my love and gratitude for all the times you've been my protector, my guide, and my friend. Happy Bhai Dooj, Dear Brothers!
